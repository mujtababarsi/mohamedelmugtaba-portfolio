About Me

Compiling and interpreting bioinformatics information by leveraging software, statistics and data mining. Effective at maintaining current understanding of field trends, technological advances and new computational and modeling techniques. specializing in single cell analysis.

With several years of experience in Pharmaceuticals in general, it's time for me to expand my drug discovery and bioinformatics knowledge outside drug manufacturing to have a real impact on health science utilize cuttingedge technologies. I believe bioinformatics can integrate my passion for technology and data analysis and my concrete background in pharmacy. Enthusiastically pursued self-guided learning in software technologies, particularly Python, to explore their applications in bioinformatics. Actively engaged in data analysis courses offered by NBIS (National Bioinformatics Infrastructure Sweden), significantly expanding my analytical skills and knowledge to a very advanced. Nevertheless elevate my skills on Linux and R-rstudio and others software tools, and practice it on real data and projects and shared on Github for better communications with Bioinformatics communities and receiving advices. My goal is to effectively leverage my computational skills and contribute to building an innovative and creative work environment.
